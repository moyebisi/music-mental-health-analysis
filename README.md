## Project : Music & Mental Health Dashboard

![Music and Mental Health Dashboard](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/moyebisi/mental-health-analysis/main/music-mental-health-dashboard.png)

An exploration of whether favourite music genre relates to anxiety, depression, insomnia, and OCD levels, using survey data from 736 respondents across 16 music genres.

**Key Findings:**
- Folk music listeners report the highest average anxiety levels; Latin listeners report the lowest
- Lofi listeners report the highest average depression levels; Gospel listeners report the lowest
- Lofi and Gospel listeners report the highest insomnia levels; Rap listeners report the lowest
- Gospel music consistently shows the lowest mental health challenges across all four indicators

**File:** `mxmh_survey_results.xlsx`
